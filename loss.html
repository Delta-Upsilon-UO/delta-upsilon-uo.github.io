Vice President of Loss Prevention

Fintan Doyle

The vice president risk management serves on the executive council and works closely with them to help prevent and reduce risk within the chapter. He educates the entire membership on Fraternity policy and loss prevention in general. He provides regular reports to the chapter and to the Fraternity concerning risk reduction and ensures that the chapter operates under guidelines established by federal, state, local, university, and Fraternity officials.
